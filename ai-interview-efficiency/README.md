## AI Interview Efficiency Report

This analysis compares traditional hiring interviews with AI-powered
interview workflows to measure efficiency gains and process improvements.

### Key metrics compared
- Time to schedule interviews
- Recruiter time per candidate
- Candidate drop-off rate
- Estimated cost per interview

### Key takeaway
AI-powered interview workflows significantly reduce time, cost,
and candidate drop-off by minimizing manual coordination and friction.
